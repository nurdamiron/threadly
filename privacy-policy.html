Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

1. What We Collect

Gardina Bot accesses your Threads account data including your user ID, published posts, and replies — solely to automate publishing and respond to comments on your behalf.

2. How We Use the Data

  • Publishing posts to your Threads account
  • Reading and replying to comments using AI
  • No data is sold or shared with third parties

3. Data Storage

Access tokens are stored securely and used only to interact with the Threads API. We do not store the content of your posts or replies beyond what is needed to operate the bot.

4. Revoking Access

You can revoke the app's access at any time through your Threads account settings. Upon revocation, all stored tokens are deleted.
